Brief Bio

I am currently a research engineer in Computer Science at Télécom Management School in Paris. I am also a lecturer at Leonard de Vinci Engineering School where I teach Machine Learning, Probability and Statistics.

Previously, I was a post-doctoral researcher at LURPA, ENS Cachan. I obtained my PhD (Nov. 2013) in Computer Science from Université Paris 13, where I worked in A3 team of LIPN under the supervision of Professor Younès Bennani.
